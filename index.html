Spirometry




Definitions

Spirometry "a breathing test which involve breathing fully in and blowing out as fast as possible"

FEV1 "after breathing in fully, the amount of air in liters forcefully expelled in one second"

FVC "after breathing in fully, the total amount of air in liters forcefully expelled"

Post-bronchodilator "after using a bronchodilator"

bronchodilator "a medication that opens airways in the lungs making it easier to breath"
